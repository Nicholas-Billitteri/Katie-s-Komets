Welcome to the Katie's Komets Website

Katie's Komets is a wheelchair basketball team for children with special needs. The organization is for students in kindergarten through high school. A youngster does not have to be a full-time wheelchair user or even own a wheelchair to participate in the program. All that is required is that the youngster has a long-term physical disability that precludes him or her from participating in a regular basketball program and that he or she is capable of using a manual wheelchair.

The National Wheelchair Basketball Association represents the team. On Saturdays, we practice in the Carousel House in Fairmount Park, Philadelphia. Our games take place on the road. Katie's Komets have toured throughout Virginia, New Jersey, New York, Maryland, Alabama, and other states. Katie's Komets also competed at nationals in Louisville, Kentucky, where they finished 22nd in the nation in April 2014 and 5th in the national NIT division in 2015. Every January, Katie's Komets hosts a regional competition in Philadelphia, with teams traveling from as far as Florida and Indiana.
